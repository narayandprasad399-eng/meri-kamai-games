About Us

Humara Mission

Meri Kamai ek skill-based mobile gaming platform hai jo India ke youth ko entertaining games ke zariye real life skills sikhata hai.

Hum believe karte hain ki games sirf timepass nahi honi chahiye — woh aapko better decision maker, better thinker, aur mentally strong bana sakti hain.

Ye Games Kyun Bane?

Aaj ke time me log social media pe ghante waste karte hain par kuch naya nahi seekhte. Humne socha — agar entertainment aur growth ek saath mil sake, to kyun nahi?

Isliye hum ne 5 alag games design kiye — har ek aapke dimaag ka ek alag corner train karta hai.

Ads Ke Baare Mein

Haan, humare games mein ads hain. Ye platform free hai aur ads se hi hum ise chalate hain. Ads sirf game ke beech mein aate hain — gameplay disturb nahi hoti.

Aapka data safe hai. Hum koi personal data collect nahi karte beyond basic analytics.
